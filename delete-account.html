Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. You can delete your account directly from the app, or by contacting us via email.

Option 1 — Delete from App (Recommended)

1

Open the PC Expert app on your device

2

Go to Profile or Settings

3

Tap "Delete Account" option

4

Confirm deletion — your account will be permanently deleted

Option 2 — Request via Email

1

Send an email to pc.expertapp@gmail.com

2

Subject: Delete My Account Request

3

Include your registered email address in the message

4

We will process your request within 7 business days

What Data Gets Deleted

  • Your account and profile information
  • Chat history and support tickets
  • Points balance and transaction history
  • All personal data stored in our systems

⚠️ Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Any unused points will be forfeited.
